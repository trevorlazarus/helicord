Helicord Transmissions Private Limited

Helicord Transmissions Private Limited (Formerly Samsons Rubber Industries Private Limited) was established in 1972 in India with an objective to manufacture and market Vee and Fan Belts under the brand name "HELICORD". Today HELICORD is a specialist in Automotive Timing Belts, Poly VBelts (Multi ribbed V Belts) and Raw Edge V Belts in Cogged, Plain and Laminated constructions.
